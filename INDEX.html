Store History
Gonesto Sari-Sari Store was established on June 20, 2024, in Granada, Boljoon, Cebu. It began as a small neighborhood store with the goal of providing convenient access to daily necessities, snacks, and drinks for the local community. Over time, the store has become a trusted and well-loved spot for residents, known for its friendly service, affordable prices, and a wide variety of products. Today, it continues to grow while staying committed to being “Iyong Suking Tindahan, Anytime.”

The Life of Shirley Gonesto
Shirley Gonesto is the dedicated owner of Gonesto Sari-Sari Store, a small yet essential business within the community. Her commitment to providing reliable and accessible goods has earned her the trust and respect of customers. She started the store with limited resources, motivated by a desire to support her family and serve the needs of her neighbors. Through consistent effort, discipline, and strong management, she has built a stable and welcoming store environment. Despite the challenges of running a sari-sari store, Shirley continues to demonstrate professionalism, compassion, and perseverance—qualities that reflect the hardworking spirit of Filipino entrepreneurs.
Success Story of:
Gonesto Sari-Sari Store
Gonesto Sari-Sari Store began as a small neighborhood shop selling basic goods. Through hard work, friendly service, and affordable prices, more customers started visiting. Over time, the store expanded its products and became a trusted place for families in the community. Today, Gonesto Sari-Sari Store continues to grow, proving that dedication and good service can turn a simple business into a successful one.
Challenges Met by Shirley Gonesto, Owner of Gonesto Sari-Sari Store
Shirley Gonesto faced many challenges while managing Gonesto Sari-Sari Store. One of the biggest problems was keeping enough stocks, especially when suppliers increased prices or deliveries were delayed. She also struggled with competition from nearby stores and convenience shops that offered more items. Managing finances was another challenge, because she had to balance earnings, restocking, and daily expenses. Despite these difficulties, Shirley remained patient, hardworking, and dedicated. Her persistence helped the store continue serving the community.
How I See My Business 10 Years After – GONESTO SARI-SARI STORE
Ten years from now, I see GONESTO SARI-SARI STORE as a bigger and more successful business in our community. The store will be well-organized, clean, and fully stocked with different products that customers need every day. I imagine adding new services like e-loading, GCash/Maya payments, and possibly a small mini-grocery section. The business will be known for its good customer service, fair prices, and trustworthiness. Through hard work, proper management, and adapting to new trends, GONESTO SARI-SARI STORE will continue to grow and provide stable income for my family while serving the community well.

The Philippine government helped GONESTO SARI-SARI STORE grow by offering small business trainings and livelihood programs through the DTI and the local government. These supports helped improve business skills, management, and daily operations.
